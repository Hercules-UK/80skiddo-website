Our Background

80s Kiddo Ltd is founder-led by someone with experience across ecommerce, CRM, customer data platforms, marketing automation, personalisation, and digital product delivery. Our work spans major UK and US retailers, ecommerce platforms, and technology companies across multiple verticals.

Our Approach

Our work focuses on turning complex business and customer problems into clear, practical solutions. We don't build for complexity's sake. Every product, service, and brand we touch has to solve something real for someone real.

How We Work

We operate as a focused, founder-led organisation. That means direct access, clear communication, and full accountability. No layers, no handoffs to junior teams, no diluted output.

Our Philosophy

We believe technology should feel human.

01

Simplicity

The best solutions are clear, not clever. We strip away what doesn't serve the person using the product, and keep what does.

02

Creativity

Good thinking comes from combining things that don't usually sit together. We bring creative instinct to every technical problem.

03

Meaningful Impact

We measure success by whether something changes behaviour, not by whether it ships. Impact over output, every time.
